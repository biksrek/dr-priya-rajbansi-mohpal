About My Practice

A Tradition of Excellence in Healthcare

Welcome to my private practice. I am Dr. Priya Rajbansh Mohpal, a Dermatologist with qualifications including MBBS Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery, MD - Dermatology.
